Chapter 1: Introduction to HRM

The Strategic Role of Human Resource Management

Learning Objectives

  • Define Human Resource Management and its core functions.
  • Understand the importance of Human Capital in modern organizations.
  • Identify the changing environment of HR (Technology, Globalization, Diversity).
  • Explain the role of HR in strategic planning.

Key HR Functions

  • Staffing: Recruitment and Selection.
  • Development: Training and Performance Management.
  • Compensation: Rewards, Benefits, and Pay Structures.
  • Employee Relations: Safety, Health, and Labor Relations.

Current Trends in HRM

Modern HR departments are shifting from administrative "paper-pushers" to strategic partners. Key drivers include:

  • Data Analytics (People Analytics)
  • Remote Work and Gig Economy Management
  • Focus on Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI)
  • Sustainability and Corporate Social Responsibility
